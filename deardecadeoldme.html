Personal Game Project "Dear Decade-old Me"

A beautifully crafted 3D narrative game that allows players to assume different roles and experience the story from various perspectives. It features an explorable map, interactive NPCs and items, an engaging main storyline, and unlockable multiple endings and CGs.

The game was showcased at the 2024 UK Develop Game Expo and received widespread acclaim.

Demo PV

Despite the continuous progress of 21st-century society, many teenagers around the world, especially females, still suffer from non-violent forms of school bullying due to their appearance, skin color, and other issues. The reasons behind this phenomenon are complex, including but not limited to gender inequality, widespread but incorrect social norms, lack of proper family education for some teenagers, low self-esteem, loss of self-identity, and incomplete values. This type of bullying, while manifesting in verbal or behavioral attacks, has caused countless young girls to lose their lives or suffer from lifelong psychological trauma, stripping them of their ability to recognize their own identity.


Dear Decade-old Me tells a story about school bullying, while also revealing the discrimination and difficulties faced by women of different ages over the past decade. Players will take on different roles and gain insights into the truth behind the female protagonist's suicide due to bullying from various perspectives. Throughout the game, players will attempt to make changes, challenge fixed mindsets, and continuously make new decisions through cycles of repetition to alter a predetermined fate.


Through this game project, I hope players can become aware of the enormous harm caused by such social issues, clearly recognize false social norms, learn not to follow them blindly, maintain a critical mind, and actively resist them. For players who have experienced similar situations or those who feel confused when facing such problems, I hope they can regain confidence and a sense of self-identity through the game, reconcile with their past, face life positively, and live in the present.

From a technical perspective, the project aims to showcase the passage of time and the process of reincarnation through interactive gameplay. The story and CG content unfold progressively in a fixed sequence, with these CG scenes interwoven into the dialogue between the player and NPCs. Different player choices will trigger different game options and endings, while also implementing features like interactive game items and a backpack system.


Game Functionality Overview

R1 - The game features third-person perspective gameplay, where the camera consistently follows the player character.
R2 - The player character can move freely within the game environment along the x and z axes, with the ability to jump along the y axis.
R3 - The player character can walk and run, providing different speeds for exploration.
R4 - The player can control different characters as the story progresses, each offering unique perspectives on the game’s narrative.
R5 - The game includes interactive dialogues with NPCs, allowing players to engage in conversations that influence the storyline.
R6 - Players can interact with key items within the game environment to obtain information and collect them in a backpack system. Once all key items are collected, the player can advance to the next level.
R7 - When certain story events are triggered, the game unlocks various CG (computer graphics) images that enhance the narrative experience.
R8 - Players can make decisions throughout the game that lead to different endings, adding replay value.
R9 - The game includes dynamic background music that changes to match the atmosphere of each scene, enhancing the overall immersive experience.
