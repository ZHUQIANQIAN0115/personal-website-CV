Project Experience

As the planner and director of this project, I was responsible for overseeing every aspect of the live show from conceptualization to execution. This experience not only provided me with valuable hands-on experience but also enhanced my teamwork and leadership abilities. In this complex project with numerous team members from diverse national and cultural backgrounds, I successfully resolved team conflicts and avoided potential disagreements. Moreover, I ensured that each person was assigned to roles that best matched their strengths, maximizing the team’s overall effectiveness.

Pre-production planning: Determined the overall direction, content structure, and target audience of the show, creating a detailed project plan.

Footage shooting: Organized and coordinated the shooting of various materials, ensuring the quality of all footage met the show's requirements.

Content creation: Designed and produced key assets, including the show’s logo, graphic materials, and transition animations, establishing a unique visual identity. Stage design: Designed a functional and aesthetically pleasing stage layout tailored to the show’s needs, enhancing the audience’s viewing experience.

Camera placement: Strategically positioned cameras to capture the best angles, heightening the intense atmosphere of the live show.

Storyboard and camera movement design: Carefully crafted shot lists and camera movements to enhance visual storytelling and pacing.

Show flow design: Planned and optimized the program flow, ensuring smooth transitions between segments.

Team coordination and collaboration: Successfully mitigated conflicts arising from cultural differences within the team, assigning roles suited to each member's strengths, which enhanced team cooperation and efficiency.

Live broadcasting and backstage command: Directed the live broadcast, managing real-time adjustments and control to ensure the success of the show, while honing my leadership skills by efficiently managing and directing the team during unforeseen challenges.
